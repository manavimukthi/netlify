ABOUT

Who we are

CEYLIZ is a modern business automation platform designed for startups and small businesses. We help you work smarter not harder by using AI tools that simplify daily tasks, save time, and boost productivity. From managing customers to sending emails or handling data, we make automation easy for everyone, even without coding. Our goal is simple: empower every entrepreneur to grow confidently in the digital world.

SERVICES

What we do

We provide automation solutions that help your business run smoothly from day one. Whether you’re building your brand, handling clients, or analyzing data CEYLIZ connects it all in one smart system.

  • AI Workflows – Automate repetitive tasks like form submissions, emails, and lead tracking.
  • Data Sync – Connect tools like Google Sheets, Gmail, and CRMs effortlessly.
  • Website Automation – Integrate your site forms, chat, and analytics without coding.

Let us handle the tech, so you can focus on what really matters — your business.
