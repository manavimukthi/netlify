ABOUT CEYLIZ

Our Story

Every small business owner faces the same challenge — too much work and not enough time. That’s where CEYLIZ was born.

We saw small business owners spending hours on emails, invoices, and spreadsheets — tasks that could easily be automated. So, in 2025, we created CEYLIZ with one goal: Make business automation easy, affordable, and stress-free for everyone.

Today, CEYLIZ helps startups, freelancers, and small businesses automate their daily work and grow faster — without coding or big budgets.

What is CEYLIZ

CEYLIZ is a smart business automation platform that brings all your daily tools together in one simple dashboard. With CEYLIZ, you can send emails, manage customers, track sales, and build automation workflows — all in a few clicks.

We make technology work for people — not the other way around.

Our Mission

To empower small businesses with intelligent, easy-to-use automation tools that help them save time and focus on what truly matters — growth and creativity.

Our Vision

We believe every business, big or small, should have access to AI and automation. Our vision is to make CEYLIZ the most trusted automation platform for small businesses worldwide.

Our Promise

  • Affordable: Fair pricing with flexible plans
  • Secure: Your data stays private and protected
  • Smart: Always improving with AI and user feedback
